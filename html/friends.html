Rainbow Dash

The bearer of the Element of Loyalty. A speedy Pegasus hailing from Cloudsdale who loves to race and compete whenever she can convince someone to go against her. Though Prideful and a bit arrogant, with a somewhat self-centered attitude, she's a loyal friend and a strong leader. Works as a weather manager of Ponyville.

Applejack

Applejack is strong, reliable, Independent and honest. She seems to be fun, playful and jokes off at times with her friends for fun. Applejack is a light orange earth pony with green eyes. She has three eyelashes on each eye that curve upward. The inside of her ears are light pink, and her fetlocks are the same color as her coat. Her mane is blonde, with a slightly curly forelock and the rest of her mane tied in a red ponytail holder. Her tail is also held in a red ponytail holder. She wears a tan cowboy hat at all times. Her cutie mark is a red apple with a green stem.

Flattershy

Fluttershy is a light yellow pegasus with aqua eyes. She has one long eyelash on each eye that curves upward. The inside of her ears are light pink, and her fetlocks are the same color as her coat. She has a long pink mane that curls at the tips, and a tail that curls similarly. Her cutie mark is an butterfly with an aqua body and pink wings.
